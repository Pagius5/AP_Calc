# 10.2 Working with Geometric Series
!!! abstract "Key Term: Common Ratio ($r$)"
    The fixed multiplier between successive terms.

!!! tip "Definition: Geometric Series Test"
    For the series $\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} ar^n$:
    
    1. If $|r| < 1$, the series **converges**. The sum is $S = \frac{a}{1-r}$.
    
    2. If $|r| \ge 1$, the series **diverges**.

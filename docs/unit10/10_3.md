# 10.3 The $n^{th}$ Term Test for Divergence

!!! tip "Definition: $n^{th}$ Term Test"
    If $\lim_{n \to \infty} a_n \neq 0$ or if the limit does not exist, then the series $\sum a_n$ **diverges**.
    
    *Note: If the limit is 0, the test is **inconclusive**; the series might converge or diverge.*

# 10.6 Comparison Tests for Convergence

!!! tip "Definition: Direct Comparison Test"
    Suppose $0 \le a_n \le b_n$ for all $n$:
    
    1. If the "larger" series $\sum b_n$ converges, then the "smaller" series $\sum a_n$ **converges**.
    
    2. If the "smaller" series $\sum a_n$ diverges, then the "larger" series $\sum b_n$ **diverges**.

!!! tip "Definition: Limit Comparison Test"
    Suppose $a_n > 0$ and $b_n > 0$. 
    
    If $\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{a_n}{b_n} = L$, where $0 < L < \infty$, then both series $\sum a_n$ and $\sum b_n$ share the same behavior (both converge or both diverge).
